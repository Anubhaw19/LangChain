Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly evolving field that focuses on creating machines capable of mimicking human intelligence. From voice assistants and recommendation engines to self-driving cars and advanced medical diagnostics, AI is transforming the way we live and work. At its core, AI involves training algorithms to recognize patterns, make decisions, and solve problems, often using large volumes of data. Subfields like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision have made significant strides in recent years, allowing machines to understand speech, analyze images, and even generate human-like text. As AI becomes more integrated into daily life, it also raises important questions about ethics, privacy, and job displacement.
